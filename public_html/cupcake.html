Background on our Cupcakes

Our cupcakes are our highest selling items! Recipes have been created and perfected by our highly skilled bakers for over 10 years. Never too sweet, our cupcakes are inspired by our favorite deserts and are always a big hit! These beautiful and delicious creations are perfect for events like:

  • Graduation Parties
  • Bridal Showers
  • Baby Showers
  • Birthday Parties
  • Weddings
  • Office Parties
  • Anniversaries

We are always creating new and exciting flavors, so visit our bakery soon to try them all! Also keep an eye out for more information about a cupcake design competition coming soon!
