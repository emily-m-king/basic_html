Background on our Pies

We base our pies off of a 200 year old recipe from Belgium. Each pie takes over 2 hours to prepare and bake. You might have seen our pies at prestigious events like Iowa county and state fairs. Our cherry pie has won many awards at these events. In order to create the highest quality pies, we only use the freshest of ingredients and source 80% of our fruit from local farmers.
